    About iCommute

    iCommute: A Mobile Commuter's Map is a Project developed by the students of FEU East-Asia College. The purpose of this Application is to aide the commuters in the event of the unfamiliar with the place and does not know how to reach the destination. The Application provide a route displaying to the map on how to go to their destination and it will display the what mode of transportation will be use and the estimated time and cost of each travel.
